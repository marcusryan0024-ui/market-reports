Strat Combos
2-1-2 Continuation
2↑ → 1 (inside coil) → 2↑ same direction. Most common setup. Wait for the inside bar to trigger before entry.
2-1-2 Reversal
2↓ → 1 (coil) → 2↑ opposite direction. Reversal off a key level. Needs higher TF FTFC for conviction.
3-2-2
Outside bar (range expansion) → two directional bars same direction. Powerful continuation. Enter on the second 2 trigger.
1-2-2 Continuation
Inside bar (compression) → two directional bars same direction. Classic breakout from a coil. Volume should expand on the break.
2-2 Reversal
2↓ → 2↑ back the other way. Counter-trend. Strongest when the first 2 is also a Failed bar on a higher TF.
Rev Strat (3-2U / 3-2D)
Outside bar at key S/R → directional bar takes out one side. Enter on the break. High conviction reversal signal.
Bullish Kicker
Bear close → gap up → bull open with no body overlap. Institutional demand. Highest conviction reversal in The Strat.
Candle Types (The Strat)
1
Inside Bar
Price coiling inside the prior bar. Market is undecided — sit on your hands until one side breaks.
2↑
Directional Up
Takes out prior High, NOT prior Low. Pure bullish pressure. Trigger = break of this bar’s high on the next bar.
2↓
Directional Down
Takes out prior Low, NOT prior High. Pure bearish pressure. Trigger = break of this bar’s low on the next bar.
3
Outside Bar
Takes out BOTH prior High and prior Low. Full range expansion. No directional edge — wait for the next bar to commit to a side.
F2U
Failed 2 Up
Exceeds prior High then reverses and closes back inside. Bulls trapped. Failed high = resistance. Bearish reversal signal.
F2D
Failed 2 Down
Exceeds prior Low then reverses and closes back inside. Bears trapped. Failed low = support. Bullish reversal signal.
BK
Bullish Kicker
Bear close → gap up → bull open with NO body overlap. Institutional demand. Highest-conviction reversal in The Strat.
